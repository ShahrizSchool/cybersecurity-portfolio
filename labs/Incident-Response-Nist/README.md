# NIST Cybersecurity Framework Incident Response – ICMP DoS Attack

## Objective
Apply the NIST Cybersecurity Framework (CSF) to analyze a denial-of-service (DoS) attack and develop a structured incident response and recovery plan.

## Scenario
A multimedia company experienced a network outage caused by a flood of ICMP packets. The attack exploited an unconfigured firewall, which allowed malicious traffic to overwhelm the internal network and disrupt services for two hours.

## NIST CSF Phases Applied
This lab follows the five NIST Cybersecurity Framework functions:

- Identify – Determine vulnerabilities and impacted assets
- Protect – Implement safeguards to prevent future attacks
- Detect – Improve monitoring and detection capabilities
- Respond – Contain and mitigate the incident
- Recover – Restore services and return to normal operations
